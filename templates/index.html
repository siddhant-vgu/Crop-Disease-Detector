About Crop Disease Detector

Revolutionizing agriculture through AI-powered disease detection

Project Introduction

Crop Disease Detector is an innovative AI-powered solution designed to help farmers identify crop diseases early and accurately. Our system combines computer vision, machine learning, and real-time environmental monitoring to provide comprehensive crop health management. By uploading simple leaf images, farmers can instantly detect diseases and receive actionable treatment recommendations.
